Istanbul Fintech Week '24

Welcome to Istanbul Fintech Week '24! Join us for an exciting week filled with innovation, networking, and learning in the heart of Istanbul. On April 16-17, 2024, in the vibrant venue of Fi≈üekhane, this in-person event is a must-attend for fintech enthusiasts.

Discover the latest trends and advancements in the fintech industry through engaging talks, panel discussions, and interactive workshops. Immerse yourself in the dynamic atmosphere as industry experts and thought leaders share their insights and experiences.

Network with like-minded professionals, entrepreneurs, and investors from around the globe. Exchange ideas, forge new partnerships, and explore potential collaborations that can drive your fintech ventures forward.

Whether you are a seasoned professional or just starting your journey in fintech, Istanbul Fintech Week '24 offers valuable opportunities to expand your knowledge, gain inspiration, and stay ahead in this rapidly evolving landscape.

IFW hosted over 11,000 participants from around 50 countries in its first four editions. IFW curates two main summits and several parallel programs during the week, including Web3 Summit and Digital Finance Summit, AI in Finance, Women in Fintech, Pioneers of Digital Finance Awards, C-Suite Roundtables, Tech Stream, Startup Pitches, Country Stages, and side events by our partners. Besides excellent content, the B2B platform will help you organize your meetings before the event.

IFW continues to foster the innovation ecosystem in the region by bringing industry practitioners and enthusiasts together from across the world.
