Cappadox

Cappadox offers a multi-disciplinary platform where people can experience contemporary art, gastronomy and outdoor activities inspired by the layered cultural texture and unique landscape of Cappadocia; In its fifth edition, it will be held between 23-26 May with the theme of "Changing Skies". Under the main sponsorship of Volkswagen, with the support of Yapı Kredi and +1; With the hospitality partnership of Doğuş Hospitality, Argos in Cappadocia, Under Armor and KIKO Milano, you will be reunited with experiences where four different disciplines come together.

The festival, which was first held in 2015 under the title "Cappadocia Impact", was held with the theme "Let's Plant Our Garden" in 2016, "Ways Out of the World" in 2017 and "Silence" in 2018. Turkey's first destination festival, Cappadox; During the past four editions, the program took place in different villages, towns and valleys of Cappadocia, with more than 80 concerts with the participation of approximately 500 musicians, 72 projects and contemporary art events with the participation of 50 contemporary artists, 50 gourmet tasting and gastronomy experiences offered by four chefs and more than 100 He met with nearly 50 thousand Cappadox members by holding an open-air event on .

The contemporary art program of the festival, organized this year under the theme of "Changing Skies", will be organized in memory of curator Fulya Erdemci, the founder and shaper of the Cappadox Contemporary Art Program, who passed away in 2022, and will host its audience until June 13. Cappadox invites participants to look at what we have learned together from Cappadocia over the past four editions. Cappadox, which takes its title from Erdemci's exhibition "Regrets, Dreams and Changing Skies" held at Karşı Sanat in 2001, aims to remember together the common experiences and possible encounters established by advancing step by step through the multi-layered historical, cultural and physical geography of Cappadocia with its participants, calls for rethinking after six years.

Embracing innovation, we craft unique experiences that resonate deeply. Our mission: fostering connections, elevating interactions. Join us on this journey where every detail inspires and delights
